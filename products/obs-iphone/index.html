üé• What is it?

We've created a plugin that allows you to use your iPhone as a video camera over USB. We've tried all the apps that let you do it over wifi, but they're not fast enough.

I got into streaming a year or so ago, and purchased the universally recommended Logitech C920 HD Pro webcam. Unfortunately, I found that the picture wasn't as good as what I expected, and my iPhone 6 camera is significantly better.

So I set out to create a plugin in OBS that would fix these two issues, and allow me to use my iPhone 6 as a video input device into OBS.

iPhone for OBS lets you use your iPhone camera to stream 1080p (up to 4k if your computer is fast enough!) directly to OBS over a USB connection.

  • iOS10+
  • Stream up to 4k direct from your iPhones camera (if your computer is fast enough!)
  • Less input lag than equivalent solutions that stream video over Wifi
  • Works in OBS running on macOS only.
