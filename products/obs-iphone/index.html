Use your iPhone camera as a video source in OBS Studio

Stream high quality video from your iPhone's camera

If you've ever wanted to use your iPhone camera as a video input to stream using OBS Studio, we've got you covered. We've tried all the apps that let you do it over wifi, but they're not fast enough, so we've created an app that streams video over a wired USB connection to your mac.
