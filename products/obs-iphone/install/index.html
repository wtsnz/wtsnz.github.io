Getting Started: Using the plugin (v1.x.x)

Using the OBS iOS Camera plugin is easy. Once you have the plugin installed and the iOS app installed:

  1. Download and install version 1.x.x of the OBS Studio iOS Camera Plugin
  2. Open OBS Studio on your computer.
  3. Add a new "iOS Camera" source to your scene.
  4. Open the OBS Stream App on your iPhone.
  5. Connect your iOS device to your computer.
  6. Use your iOS device as a webcam and stream your heart out! ðŸ˜ƒ

Plugging in your iOS device with the app running automatically connects to OBS Studio.
